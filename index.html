About Us

We are a small business founded by plant enthusiasts. Like many, our busy lives often prevented us from taking care of our plants adequately. We often wished that there was a service that watered our plants when we were on vacation or helped us understand and remedy our plants' ailments. When we couldn't find anything like this, we decided to create our own business. As forever plant addicts, we now spend our days pampering plants for people like you!
